1. What is the problem you are solving?
> Millions of Africans face hunger every year but nobody has a clear picture of where it's worst, why it happens and how to prepare for it. In Kenya, food aid always arrives after people are already starving. This project uses data to show where hunger is happening, why it keeps happening and how we can see it coming before it hits.

3. What insights did you or do you want to discover?/ What Solutions do you want to offer? Do People even need these solutions?
> Which African countries are most food insecure and is it getting better or worse?
> Is poverty the main cause of hunger or are there other factors?
> Which counties in Kenya are in crisis and why?
> Can we predict when the next food shortage will happen?

> The biggest "aha!" moments:
- Kenya produces enough food — the problem is getting it to the right places
- Drought and conflict hurt more than poverty in northern Kenya
- Every drought is followed by a food crisis 3—6 months later — we can see it coming


4. How would a business or a community (for Social Impact Projects) benefit from your work?
> Government — know where to send aid before a crisis, not after
> NGOs — stop wasting resources, send help where it's needed most
> Farmers — know which crops survive in their region and when risk is highest
> Communities — plan ahead, stock food before the hard months hit
